The Relic Starbow Experience is an acoustic rock duo from Northeast Pennsylvania, featuring Bill and Candi—two seasoned local musicians who have come together to deliver an engaging and memorable live performance. With a diverse setlist spanning from the 1960s through the 2000s, their shows offer a blend of timeless favorites and crowd-pleasing classics. If you’re looking for an evening filled with familiar sing-alongs and an upbeat atmosphere, be sure to catch an upcoming performance. We look forward to seeing you there.
