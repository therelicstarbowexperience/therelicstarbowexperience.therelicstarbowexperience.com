The Relic Starbow Experience is an acoustic rock duo from northeast Pennsylvania featuring Bill and Candi, two long time local musicians who have joined together to create good times with good tunes. Featuring a diverse and familiar list of songs from the 60's to the 2000's, the night is always sure to be a great time. If you're looking to get in on a fun evening with familiar sing alongs and happy vibes, check out an upcoming show! We look forward to seeing you there!
